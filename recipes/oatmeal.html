Oatmeal

Description

This Mexican oatmeal brings back memories of when my mother made it for us when we were kids. Made with milk, cinnamon, and vanilla, itâ€™s sweet, creamy, and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 Mexican cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1 tablespoon piloncillo, or to tast
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring water and cinnamon stick to a boil in a saucepan. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add oats and cook for 1 minute. Add milk and cook until thickened, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat. Stir in piloncillo, cinnamon, and vanilla.
